Building a MEAN Web Stack

Have you ever wanted to build a MEAN web stack but was never sure where to start? Well, you're in luck! This Thursday, the USC LUG will be hosting an event to teach you just how to do that. Ankit Tyagi (Software Engineer at Dun & Bradstreet Credibility Corp) and Sheldon Kwok (Software Engineer at Second Spectrum) will be having a hands-on class to setup and build your own MEAN (Mongo, Express, AngularJS, Node) web app on a FREE Amazon EC2 Micro Instance.
