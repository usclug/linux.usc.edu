About Us

We are the USC Linux Users Group (aka USCLUG) and we're an organization at the University of Southern California dedicated to creating and developing the Linux and Open-Source Community at USC. We're a group of students at USC passionate about everything Linux and Open-Source, and we'll be constantly hosting technical workshops related to Linux and software development.

There are no formal meetings for USCLUG, but if you would like to get involved or help with our workshops, get in touch with us. :)
